  tapped for their plays) but conflates lands tapped for other reasons. The
  strategist only surfaces it descriptively in the prompt, so the rough proxy is
   fine — but if you want exact mana-spent tracking, that needs per-turn
  accounting in the observer, which is a larger change.

  Design note

  I deliberately did not pre-enumerate the combination space (your WW, WU, WB...
   example) on either side — for N sources across C colors that's exponential,
  and the LLM reasons about combinations better from the compact per-source
  options. The strategist sees "2 mana, sources [W/U] and [W/U/B/R/G], reachable
   W/U/B/R/G" and works out castability itself, combined with its hand read.

  One known limitation: each source is modeled as producing one mana. Lands that
   tap for 2+ (e.g. Ancient Tomb, bounce lands) undercount total mana. If that
  matters for your meta I can fold in getMaxManaProduced() per source — say the
  word.
